Obsessions

In the grand scheme of life, some things consume us, which we willingly let devour our free time and become so engrossed that we lose track of the hours. These are what I like to call my idle time obsessions. They're the interests that take center stage, the passions that dictate how I spend my sparse leisure hours.

So, how do I determine what my current idle time obsession is? It's simple. I ask myself, "If I had abundant free time only for myself, what would I do now?" The answer to that question is the answer.
